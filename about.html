About Me

My name is Ruby O’Keeffe.

I am a current student at Parsons, The New School, in pursuit of an MFA in Design and Technology. I previously studied at Brown University (2024), holding a BA in English Literature and Gender and Sexuality Studies.

Outside of term time, I am based between London (United Kingdom) and Melbourne (Australia). I am a passionate UX/UI designer, graphic designer, copywriter and editor, content creator, and social media developer. I am interested in the intersection between digital design and queer culture, and the development of inclusive, empowering digital spaces in general.

When I’m not working you can find me creating experimental green-screen footage, DJing, or making more iPad artwork.

Professional skills: Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Lightroom); Figma; Cargo, Squarespace, and Wix; HTML and CSS.
